Angstromers Edu Services

Angstromers Edu. Services is an entity of Angstromers committed to nurture young talent through structured internship programs and projects. These initiatives are designed not only to provide real-world experience but also to guide students in transforming their engineering expertise to industry required skills for onboarding. We enable them to publish research papers and develop POCs, thereby bridging the gap between academic learning and scholarly contribution.

One of our recent initiatives is the 100-hour course on RISCV and its applications. We offer extensive training in RISCV with the following domains:

  1. Software Approach with RISCV Simulators
  2. Firmware development with RISC V (Soft Core) on FPGA
  3. Hardware development with RISC V Hard Core (ASIC)
